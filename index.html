About

From the early age of 11, I developed a deep passion for coding and computers. What started as curiosity quickly developed into a huge interest leading me to experimentwith various microcomputers such as the Raspberry Pi, Arduino, and Microbit. I spent hours learning how to write code and understand the intricate workings of both hardware and software inside computers.

Now, at the age of 15, I am a dedicated GCSE student with a strong focus on mathematics, physics, and computer science. My passion for coding has only grown over the years along with my ambition to pursue a career as either a software engineer or a car tuner. I find satisfaction in problem-solving and pushing the boundaries of what I can achieve.

Beyond my academic and technology intrests I also prioritize maintaining a healthy lifestyle. Twice a week , I go climbing locally which challenges both my physical and mental endurance. Additionally, since the age of 10 I have attended weekly personal training sessions helping me stay fit, disciplined, and focused.

Balancing my love for technology with an active lifestyle has made me always eager to learn, improve, and take on new challenges which come with life. I am always looking for ways to push my limits and grow overall.
